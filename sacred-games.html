Sartaj, who is a cynical and honest officer in mumbai police, is often used and left alone, because of his honesty. He lives alone because he has been divorced from his wife. One fine day, he receives a call from a person who was pretending to be Gaitonde, an underworld don (he was supposed to be dead ). He tells sartaj that whole mumbai city was going to be dead in 25 days. When sartaj rejects the idea of beleving him, he openly challenges him to look out on registers to check who he is,"Ja jaake record dekh, man insaan hai ya bhagwan". When sartaj still refuses (intentionally, just to track him down), he starts narrating his own life story of becoming underworld don He warns Sartaj about a very serious threat to mumbai,"25 din hai tere paas, bachale apne sheher ko.
